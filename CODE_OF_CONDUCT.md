# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and technically rigorous community.

Participants must:

- be respectful and constructive;
- assume good faith while challenging ideas precisely;
- keep security discussions responsible and avoid publishing live exploit targets or secrets;
- accept correction and prioritize evidence over status;
- respect privacy and confidentiality.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, malicious disclosure, and deliberate distribution of harmful credentials or payloads are not acceptable. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository’s configured security/contact channel.
